Ntfy TV Notifications - How to Delete Your Data

Important: Ntfy TV Notifications stores ALL data locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers or stored in the cloud. This means you have complete control over your data.

How Your Data is Stored

The app stores the following data locally on your Android TV device only:

  • Your ntfy.sh topic subscriptions
  • Received notification messages
  • App preferences and settings

No personal information, analytics, or usage data is collected or transmitted to any external servers (except your configured ntfy.sh connection).

How to Delete Your Data

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it directly on your device using either of these methods:

Method 1: Clear App Data (Keeps App Installed)

  1. On your Android TV, go to Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps or Applications
  3. Find and select Ntfy TV Notifications
  4. Select Clear data or Storage → Clear data
  5. Confirm the action

This will delete all subscriptions, messages, and settings while keeping the app installed.

Method 2: Uninstall the App (Removes Everything)

  1. On your Android TV, go to Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps or Applications
  3. Find and select Ntfy TV Notifications
  4. Select Uninstall
  5. Confirm the action

This will completely remove the app and all associated data from your device.

Data Deletion is Immediate

When you clear app data or uninstall the app, all data is immediately deleted from your device. There is no additional processing time or server-side deletion required because no data is stored on our servers.

What About Backups?

If you have Android's automatic backup enabled, your device may have backed up app data to your Google account. To remove this:

  1. Go to your device Settings → Google → Backup
  2. Find Ntfy TV Notifications in the backup list
  3. Remove it from backups if desired

Note: This backup is managed by Google, not by our app.
